    I'm a data scientist aiming for a role in analytics, with experience as a product data analyst at Assurant and marketing data analyst at Axis Bank. Skilled in SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, MS Excel, data storytelling, documentation, communication, and stakeholder management.
